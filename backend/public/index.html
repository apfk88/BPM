Frequently Asked Questions

How?

Step 1: Download and open app

Step 2: Connect any BLE heart rate monitor (like a Polar H10)

Step 3: Connect to the monitor in the app

Step 4: Look at your heart rate. Do great things.

Step 5: Optionally share your live heart rate with someone else like a trainer or a very close friend.

Why?

Because I couldn't find a simple one, so I made one out of spite.

Why isn't it free?

First, to cover server costs of the real-time sharing, which is terribly unoptimized and expensive. Second, to reduce the number of users and the bugs/issues they will encounter.

Where do I report bugs?

The project is open source so file an issue there or email me.
